Our Story

Born from Compassion

LifeDrop was founded on a simple but powerful belief: no one should ever have to lose a loved one because of a blood shortage. As an NGO, we bridge the gap between kind-hearted volunteers and the hospitals that rely on them.

We work directly within our local communities to spread awareness, dismiss the myths surrounding blood donation, and build a reliable registry of ready-to-help heroes.

Our Core Values

Compassion

We treat every donor and recipient with the utmost empathy and respect, recognizing the profound humanity in this process.

Integrity & Safety

We strictly align with medical science regulations. The safety of our volunteers and the confidentiality of their data is absolute.

Community First

We believe in the power of local people helping local people. A strong community is a healthy community.
